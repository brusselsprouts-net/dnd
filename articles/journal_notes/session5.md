### The Monastery
#### June 13, 2026

Despite a pretty perilous journey through the woods, the party finally sees the monastery up ahead. Stone adorned with gold glimmer in the sun, but Lachlan's mood is visibly shaken. Kaimi offers to cast Calm Emotions on him, but he declines. The party crosses the small valley and climbs up to the gates, when someone up on the wall calls out to them. The guard notices Lachlan, but does not seem to particularly reconize him. After all, Lachlan got hid of his monk garb when he escaped. Thankfully, they are assessed as non-threats and get welcomed inside.

Once inside, the inner courtyard is surrounded by smiths, forges, dormitories and a lot of monks. A group of them is training, punching into rectangular pillars, precisely in sync. Novice monks.
