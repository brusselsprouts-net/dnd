### The First Fight - <span class='name-lachlan'> Lachlan </span>'s Bedroom Adventures Aftermath
#### March 30, 2026
The story dropped off last time with <span class='name-lachlan'> Lachlan </span>'s nightly pursuits and an unexpected fire. The person that walked into his room is a bald, tall and scary-looking man looking for <span class='name-lachlan'> Lachlan </span>'s eggs. The dragon eggs that the party stole from the Dragon Mother in weeks prior. The bard gets kicked out of the room and <span class='name-lachlan'> Lachlan </span> is naked and cornered. He tries to run past the intruder, out of the room but combat ensues. 

<span class='name-sillidious'> Sillidious </span> still being around, hears the commotion and runs into the naked fleeing bard. He decides to take action, summoning his companion and walking into the burning tavern. The fire keeps spreading and the bell tower is loudly ringing to alert the city of the incident. 

The sounds reach <span class='name-talon'> Talon </span>, <span class='name-kaimi'> Kaimi </span> and <span class='name-thalia'> Thalia </span>, who somehow reluctantly make haste to go see what is going on. <span class='name-thalia'> Thalia </span> and <span class='name-kaimi'> Kaimi </span> borrow a horse that belongs to <span class='name-thalia'> Thalia </span>'s old neighbor, promising to return it soon. They start galloping towards Neverwinter when they cross paths with <span class='name-talon'> Talon </span>. He goes into rat-mode and starts scurrying down the little side streets, hurrrying towards the tavern as well. 

Meanwhile, at the tavern, <span class='name-sillidious'> Sillidious </span> and <span class='name-lachlan'> Lachlan </span> are fighting for their lives, when they come across a familiar face. The red wizard Iarno is back to terrorize the party, after they spared him last time. The party, all together again, manage to fight the fiends, but Iarno manages to escape at the last minute with one of the dragon eggs. This is not good...
